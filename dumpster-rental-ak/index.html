Frequently Asked Questions in Alaska, AK

How do I know what size dumpster I need?

We’ll ask about your project type, volume of waste, and space availability to help you select the best fit. Most home projects need a 10- or 20-yard dumpster.

Can I get same-day delivery?

Yes, we offer same-day delivery based on availability. We also guarantee next-day service across the Alaska, AK area for orders placed before 4 PM.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Standard rentals are 7 days, but extensions are available. Just let us know if your project needs more time.

What materials can I put in the dumpster?

Household junk, furniture, yard waste, construction debris, roofing materials, and non-hazardous trash are all acceptable. We’ll provide a list of restricted items (like paints or batteries) when you book.

Is there a weight limit?

Each dumpster comes with a built-in weight allowance. Overages are charged at a standard per-ton rate—no surprise fees.
